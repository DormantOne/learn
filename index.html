Passage:

Jane woke up early to spend the day at the lake. She packed her bag with snacks, a book, and a thick mat to sit on. The sun was hot, but the lake breeze was cool.

At the lake, Jane saw a man with a big hat and a long robe walking by. He waved at her, and she waved back. She felt the soft sand under her feet and smiled. She found a spot near a tall tree, set up her mat, and opened her book.

Later, Jane went for a swim. The water was cold at first, but she soon got used to it. She dove under the waves and floated for a while. The lake was calm, and the blue sky made her feel at peace.

After her swim, Jane sat on her mat again and ate some grapes. As she looked at the sky, she thought, "This is the best day."
